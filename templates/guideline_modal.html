Vocabloom Community Guidelines

The following is the list of acts that can cause limitations (permanent expulsion, temporary expulsion, and so on).

Accounts

  • Using multiple accounts
  • Using another person's account
  • Sharing your account with others
  • Not using the legal name
  • False account information (e.g. age, gender, region)
  • Inappropriate profile details

Qualifications

  • Under age 16 (Middle school students not allowed)
  • Low English level causing communication issues

Clothes

  • Underwear or nudity
  • Only white sleeveless shirt
  • Only wearing brassiere

Device Settings (Camera and Mic)

  • Unstable network
  • Camera off or not fully showing your face
  • Multiple people in frame
  • Moving screen
  • No earphones/headsets or loud background noise
  • Too dark or backlight obscuring face

Manners

  • Doing other things during 1:1 chats
  • Interrupting the host
  • Requesting personal info
  • Forcing beliefs or actions
  • Under influence of drugs/alcohol

Sensitive Content

  • Disturbing visuals
  • Hateful or violent speech
  • Sexually offensive behavior
  • Threats or intimidation
  • Sending malicious or harmful content

Misuse of the System

  • Ticket system abuse
  • False reports of users
